# Self Improvement

## Objective

Document the current self-improvement capability that enables the platform to learn from outcomes and improve future behavior in a controlled way.

## Context

The platform evolved from static execution and retrieval into a more autonomous architecture that can evaluate outcomes, reflect on experience, consolidate knowledge, and adapt future behavior.

## Core capabilities

- Evaluation: assess execution results and generate learning signals.
- Adaptation: convert validated insights into updated behavior or strategy.
- Knowledge: consolidate experience into persistent and reusable knowledge.
- Performance monitoring: observe quality, latency, reliability, and decision outcomes over time.

## Current architectural role

The self-improvement layer is positioned after autonomy and before controlled influence over future planning or routing decisions. It connects execution outcomes with explicit governance, making improvement traceable rather than reactive.
