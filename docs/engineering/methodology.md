# Engineering Methodology

## Objective

Describe the engineering approach used to evolve the platform in a structured and maintainable manner.

## Principles

- modular development
- clear separation of responsibilities
- progressive capability evolution
- documented architectural decisions
- reproducible environments and dependencies

## Current practice

The project is maintained through a combination of modular source structure, explicit architecture documents, ADRs, and testing discipline.
